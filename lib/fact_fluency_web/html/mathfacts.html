About Us

For many of us, our most precious comodity is time. Teachers are no different. Which is why a third grade teacher approached a friend to solve a problem. Fact Fluency plays a large part in your child’s success in math. The Common Core requires that students are fluent in the different operations by the end of each year. To practice, assess, and monitor a student’s progress takes time. Time that could be better spent. We’ve built this app to get your time back.

Not For Profit

Here at Fact Fluency we are a non-profit built to help teachers and parents. We practice, and assess student progress towards mastering their fact fluency. We will never take an extra dollar in prusuit of this. We just want to pay the bills.

Spice It Up

Everywhere is different. We get that. That is why we’ve made every bit fully customizable. Whether it’s practice tests or the real deal we give you all the ingredients you need to customize each test for student success.

Spice It Up

Don’t worry, we can break it down too. Every student gets a custom report that can be used to help you calculate grades or send notifications to parents. All while being beautifully plain and simple.
